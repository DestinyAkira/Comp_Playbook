Welcome to the Compliance Playbook!

Purpose of this Playbook

This Compliance Playbook serves as a comprehensive guide for ensuring adherence to data governance, privacy, and regulatory standards across all data-related activities within our organization. It outlines procedures, roles, and resources necessary to maintain a robust compliance framework.

The playbook aims to standardize our approach to data management, mitigate risks, and foster a culture of compliance. It includes an interactive audit form, a score calculator for compliance assessment, and a centralized repository for supporting documents.
