Roles & Responsibilities

A clear understanding of roles is crucial for a smooth and efficient audit. Below are the key roles and their responsibilities.

Compliance Audit Team Lead

Responsibilities: Oversees the audit process, maintains this SOP, and ensures it is reviewed and updated at least annually or after each audit cycle.
Team Member: Ross Browne

Audit Team Members

Responsibilities: Responsible for conducting audits as per this SOP, contributing to refinements based on audit findings and team feedback.
Team Members: Akira Devonish, Cherelle Griffith, Christopher Gilkes, Tansha Powlett, Tazia Holder, Tyrell McCollin, Victor Clarke, Roger Barrow

Domain Lead

Responsibilities: Expected to participate in the audit process and provide accurate and timely information during assessments.
Team Member: Terry Bennett

Domain Co-Lead

Responsibilities: Assist lead in the audit process and provide accurate and timely information during assessments.
Team Member: Destiny Holder

Data Librarian

Responsibilities: Expected to monitor work product for version control, ensure that the work product is stored appropriately and securely.
Team Member: Roger Barrow

Domain Coordinator

Responsibilities: May review SOP adherence and provide oversight or recommendations for improvement.
Team Member: Dale Edwards
